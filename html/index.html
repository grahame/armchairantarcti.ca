Armchair Antarctica
Antarctica's an amazing place. It's also a pretty expensive place to visit, and even then, you can't time travel. So, our aim for GovHack 2013 has been to create an interactive mapping interface that allows users to explore various aspects of the great, southern-most continent, both present and past. In the spirit of international collaboration driving the extraordinary Antarctic Treaty, we've also looked at the awesome data provided by New Zealand, United Kingdom, and United States agencies, and acknowledge the contribution of these agencies to Australia's own Antarctic programme. - @armchairtica
